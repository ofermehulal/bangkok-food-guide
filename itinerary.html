Backup picks

Solid spots not in the main plan. Use as swaps if anything's closed, queues are insane, or the appetite shifts.

  • Saengchai Phochana — Khlong Toei seafood, late-night. Swap for Somboon if you want grittier/cheaper.
  • Pier 21 Food Court — Terminal 21 mall. Bulletproof when tired. 5 min from hotel.
  • Rung Rueang Pork Noodle — Sukhumvit Soi 26, Michelin Bib. Backup if Wattana Panich is closed.
  • Baan E-sarn Muangyos — Isaan + beers. For a chill "long dinner" night.
  • Kuay Jab Mr Joe — Yaowarat. Peppery rolled rice noodle. Swap for Ann or Nai Ek.
  • Kam's Roast — CentralWorld, 1 Michelin star. Swap if you want roast duck Sat lunch.
  • Lay Lao — Hua Hin–style Thai, Michelin Bib 2018–2025. Swap for Somboon Friday.
  • Jae Wan — Chula comfort food. Lunch swap if you're near Sam Yan.
  • Lim Lao Ngow Fishball Noodle — Yaowarat. Old-school fishballs. Swap for Hia Big 5.
  • Lung Cheay Egg Noodles — backup if Bamee is closed.
  • Good Taste Street Food — closer-to-hotel pad krapao backup.
  • Uncle Liu — hidden Thai-Chinese stir-fry spot. Hard to find — skip unless you have the exact location.
